# YouTube Template — Video Package (Title, Description, Metadata)

Stage 04 creates YouTube package: title, description, timestamps, tags, thumbnail spec, playlist assignment.

---

# [Topic] — YouTube Package

## Video Title
**Primary:** [Topic] — [Angle/Outcome] (50-60 chars, include primary keyword)

Examples:
- Teaching: "Token Efficiency in ICM: How to Scale AI Without Cost Explosion (50+ min)"
- Enterprise: "AI Safety at Scale: How Safe AI Powers Competitive Advantage"

**Rules:**
- Lead with keyword (SEO boost)
- Keep under 60 chars (displays fully on mobile)
- Avoid ALL CAPS or excessive punctuation
- NO clickbait ("You won't BELIEVE..." "Doctors HATE this...")

---

## Video Description (500-800 chars)

**Structure:**
1. Hook (first 2 lines) — what will viewer learn?
2. Timestamps (if >10 min)
3. Resources / links
4. Playlist info
5. Subscribe CTA

**Template:**

```
[1-2 sentence hook]
In this video, you'll learn [X], understand [Y], and be able to [Z].

[RESOURCE LINKS]
📚 Research sources: [link1], [link2], [link3]
🎬 Watch the series: [playlist link]
📖 Read the blog post: [blog post link]
💬 Join the conversation: [community link, optional]

[TIMESTAMPS]
0:00 — Introduction
0:45 — Problem Setup
2:30 — What Is [Concept]?
5:15 — How It Works (Step 1)
7:45 — How It Works (Step 2)
10:00 — Real-World Example
12:30 — Why It Matters
14:15 — What You Should Do
15:00 — Recap + Next Steps

[SERIES INFO]
Part [X] of [X] in the [Series Name] series.
Previous: [video title] [link]
Next: [video title] [link]

[CTA]
✅ Subscribe for the full [Series] series
✅ Like if you learned something new
✅ Comment: What was your biggest takeaway?

#[hashtag1] #[hashtag2] #[hashtag3]
```

**Tips:**
- First 2-3 lines are all viewers see before "Show More" — make them count
- Include links to related content (blog, playlist, next video)
- Timestamps help viewers jump to sections they want
- Use emojis sparingly (breaks up text, but not excessive)
- Hashtags: 3-5 relevant tags (not spam)

## Thumbnail Specification

**Dimensions:** 1280 x 720 pixels (16:9)

**Safety area (text fits inside):** 40px margin on all sides

**Design:**
- **Hero element:** One bold visual that represents topic (icon, screenshot, illustration)
- **Text:** One short phrase (3-5 words max) — "CONTEXT EFFICIENCY" not "Understanding How Token Context Works"
- **Color:** High contrast (teaching: warm, enterprise: professional). Stand out in grid.
- **Face (if included):** Emotion matches tone (curious for teaching, confident for enterprise)

**Example specs:**

Teaching thumbnail:
- Background: Warm gradient (orange to yellow)
- Main visual: Simple icon of "context" (e.g., brain, book, hierarchy)
- Text (bold sans-serif, white with dark outline): "TOKEN EFFICIENCY"
- Emotion: Curious, approachable

Enterprise thumbnail:
- Background: Dark with professional accent color
- Main visual: Chart or system diagram showing growth/impact
- Text (bold sans-serif, bright color): "SAFE AI SCALES"
- Emotion: Confident, authoritative

**Avoid:**
- Cluttered designs (too many elements)
- Hard to read text (test at 150px width — what does it look like small?)
- Faces that look staged or fake
- Inconsistent style with other videos in series

## Subtitles / Captions

**Provide:** VTT or SRT file with full captions
- **Format:** WebVTT (.vtt file)
- **Timing:** Match to video frame-by-frame (not auto-generated if possible; auto-captions have errors)
- **Translation:** (If supporting multiple languages, provide translations)

YouTube will auto-caption, but human-corrected captions improve:
- Accessibility (deaf/HOH viewers)
- SEO (captions are searchable)
- Engagement (viewers watch longer with captions)

## Long-Term SEO

- **Backlink:** Add video link to blog post (embeds also help)
- **Social promotion:** Share to LinkedIn (enterprise), Twitter, email list
- **Community tab:** If monetized, share snippets of video with link to full video
- **Playlist order:** Ensure teaching/enterprise series are in correct sequence (affects recommendations)

---

## Tips for YouTube Success

- **Consistency:** Upload on schedule (weekly, bi-weekly, etc.). YouTube algorithm favors predictable channels.
- **Watch time:** Longer videos (12-15+ min) perform better than shorts for educational content. Hook viewers early (first 30 sec), keep them through end.
- **Engagement:** Every video should have a question or CTA that encourages comments. YouTube ranks videos higher if they have engagement.
- **Playlist power:** Group videos in series. Playlists auto-play next video, keeping viewers on channel longer.
- **Collaborations:** Cross-promote with other creators (if applicable). Mention in description, build community.

## Series Naming Convention

**Teaching stream:** "ICM [Series Name]: Zero-to-Hero" or "ICM for [Audience]"
- Example: "ICM Context Management: Zero-to-Hero" (Playlist: 10 videos in sequence)

**Enterprise stream:** "AI [Topic]: Leadership Series" or "[Topic] for Executives"
- Example: "AI Safety at Scale: Executive Series" (Playlist: 10 videos)

Keep naming consistent so viewers recognize series.
